dreamland _

COIN is an indie-pop band from Nashville, Tennessee. They released their sophomore album Dreamland in February 2020. In late 2019, they began releasing promotional material for Dreamland which is based around a bright orange color swatch. Having seen the visuals and listened to the album, I wanted to take inspiration from their visual identity and build upon it further.
