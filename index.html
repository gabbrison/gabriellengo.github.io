    How Do You Manage Your Moolah?

    Need to stay on top of your expenses and budget? Look no further! Moolah is designed to help you keep track of your daily expenses so your wallet never empties. Add expenses and income, manage your budget, and see how you manage your expenses year-to-year. It's easy to get started, just log in or sign up, it's free!

    Who We Are

    We are a small team that built this web application to help you manage your expenses in the simplest way possible. We help you do all the calculations so that you don't have to!

    What We Offer

    We offer a simple platform that allows you to easily add expenses and income. Add a budget or income goal to help you stay on track! View your expenses through different categories and visually watch your progress for the month.
