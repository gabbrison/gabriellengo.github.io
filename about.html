about

Hey there, my name is Gabrielle, but you can call me Gabby! I'm a Dallas native and a graduate from The University of Texas at Dallas. With a strong focus in design, I obtained a B.A. in Arts, Technology, and Emerging Communications with a minor in Computer Science.

I'm a photographer, music lover, and a novice film buff. You can usually find me consuming copious amounts of iced coffee and going down Wikipedia wormholes. I also dabble in writing stories and poems from time to time and am currently improving my French.

Design is a medium that fuels the universal connection of understanding and representing our world. Storytelling through design has always been my passion, no matter the medium. As a creative, I believe it is within my power to contribute to our community by breaking barriers and continuously communicating through creativity.
